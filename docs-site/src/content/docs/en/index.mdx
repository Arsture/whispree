## What Whispree does

Press a shortcut → speak → stop, and Whispree handles **dictation (STT) → (optional) AI correction → automatic paste into the app you were last using** in one go. Both the dictation engine and the correction AI are **swappable at runtime**, and everything works end to end with local models alone.

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Stay in your flow" icon="rocket">
    Default shortcut `Ctrl+Shift+R`. Press, speak, and the result lands in the previously focused app (editor, terminal, browser).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Local-first, your choice" icon="setting">
    STT runs on WhisperKit (local) · Groq (cloud) · MLX Audio (local); correction on local MLX · OpenAI · Groq · Claude subscription.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Dictation that never stops" icon="seti:audio">
    Start the next recording immediately while the previous one is still processing. Insertion follows the order you spoke (FIFO).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Understands context" icon="seti:image">
    Capture the screen during recording and pass it to a vision model, or remember the browser tab and terminal session and paste back into exactly that spot.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

## Requirements

- **macOS 14 or later**, **Apple Silicon (arm64) only** — Intel Macs are not supported.
- Microphone permission is required. Automatic paste needs Accessibility permission.
- Local models download on first use (WhisperKit STT ~1.5 GB, etc.). Cloud engines (Groq/OpenAI) work immediately once authenticated.

For detailed installation, permissions, and your first dictation, see [Get started](/en/getting-started/).
